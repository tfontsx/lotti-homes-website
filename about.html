The Family Behind Lotti Homes

More than builders.
Creators of exceptional spaces.

Lotti Homes is proudly South Australian and family-owned, specialising in custom home building, renovations and extensions across Adelaide and the surrounding region. From new builds and knockdown rebuilds to complete kitchen, bathroom and laundry renovations, every project reflects our commitment to premium craftsmanship, quality materials and lasting value.

As a locally owned Adelaide builder, we understand what it takes to create homes suited to South Australian living — from character homes in the inner suburbs to growing families along the coast. Our team manages every stage of your build in-house, from initial consultation and concept design through to final handover.

We value strong, transparent relationships with our clients, trades and the wider Adelaide community, and every fixed-price contract we deliver is backed by genuine care and meticulous attention to detail. We look forward to bringing that same dedication to your next new build, renovation or extension.
