How We Work

A considered
process, from first sketch to
final key.

From your first consultation to the final handover, our building process is designed to keep your new home, renovation or extension on brief, on budget and on-time — with clear communication at every stage. Here's how we bring Adelaide homes to life.

The Lotti Process

Seven steps, one seamless journey.

01

Initial Consultation & Discovery

Our process begins with an initial consultation to discuss your vision, budget and timeframe. It's a relaxed conversation where we listen first, so every recommendation that follows is grounded in what matters most to you.

Free, No Obligation
02

Concept Design & Specifications

Where required, we prepare concept designs to bring your brief to life. From there we obtain detailed quotations and finalise plans and specifications, so every material, finish and inclusion is documented clearly before you commit.

Where Required
03

Contract & Approvals

You're provided with a fixed-price building contract for complete cost certainty. Once approvals are in place — including council permits and any required certifications — we finalise scheduling and prepare the site so construction can begin without delay.

Your Certainty
04

Construction & Trade Coordination

We commence construction while keeping you informed throughout every stage. We coordinate all trades, manage quality control and conduct regular site inspections, with regular project updates along the way.

Build Phase
05

Final Walkthrough & Handover

Upon completion, we undertake a final walkthrough with you, addressing any outstanding items before handover. Your completed home or renovation is backed by all relevant warranties and certifications.

Completion
